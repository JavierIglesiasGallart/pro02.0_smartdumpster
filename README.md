
# SMARTDUMPSTER 2

This project proposes to automate the deployment and collection of a series of smart containers in the school playground by means of an AGV robot, facilitating the collection and recycling of the waste generated. To achieve this, the three agents involved in the VET ecosystem have been involved in order to achieve a comprehensive training of the student oriented towards the workplace: educational centre, technology centre and company.

The aim of this collaboration is to tackle a social and environmental challenge: raising awareness of the importance of reducing waste generation and promoting its recycling. The idea of designing and manufacturing an intelligent container called a "Smart dumpster" fits in perfectly with this goal. Thus, the aim is to achieve a high level of recycling of the waste generated at the centre in order to reduce the pollution ratios that are responsible for the presence of waste in the soil, water and atmosphere, as well as contributing to the current climate change, among other things.
